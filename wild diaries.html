Different types of wild animals

There are different types of wild animals in jungle that roam here and there in search of food in scorching heat

Cat is a domestic species of a small carnivorous mammal. It is the only domesticated species in the family Felidae and is often referred to as the domestic cat.

Tiger

The tiger is the largest living cat species and a member of the genus Panthera. It is most recognisable for its dark vertical stripes on orange fur with a white underside.

Lion

The lion is a large cat of the genus Panthera native to Africa and India. It has a muscular deep - chested body , short, rounded head, and a hairy tuft at the end of its tail.

Bear

Bears are carnivonian mammals of the family Ursidae . They are classified as caniforms, or dog like carnivorans. Although only eight species of bears are extant , they are widespread, appearing in a wide variety of habitats throughout the Northern hemisphere and partially in the southern hemisphere.
